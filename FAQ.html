# About The Culture Answers Reference
1 What is the traditional culture of Seoul? Seoul has a rich cultural heritage that includes Confucianism, Buddhism, and Shamanism. Traditional Korean culture can be categorized into intangible culture, tangible culture, and living culture... Ivisit Korea
2 What is the most popular food in Seoul? Seoul is known for its delicious street food, including tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes), hotteok (sweet pancakes), and kimbap (rice rolls). Britannica
3 What is the traditional dress of Seoul? The traditional dress of Seoul is called hanbok. It is a beautiful and colorful dress that is worn on special occasions such as weddings, birthdays, and festivals. Yatra
# About Entertainment Answers Reference
1 What are some popular entertainment options in Seoul? Seoul is a paradise when it comes to entertainment options. Here you will find all kinds of events, from big concerts to up-coming bands, global parties, famous musicals, dance performances, festivals, clubbing, karaoke and the list goes on. Myguideseoul
2 What is the most popular theater show in Seoul? NANTA is the longest running show in Korean history. It premiered in 1997 and is a non-verbal performance that combines traditional Korean percussion music with comedy. Trazy
